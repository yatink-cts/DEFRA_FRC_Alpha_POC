Report your forest risk commodity due diligence

If you use forest risk commodities as part of your business activity, you might be required to undertake a due diligence exercise on your supply chains, and to report on this exercise annually to evidence that those commodities were grown on land legally occupied or owned.

If your business meets the following requirements, you will be required to complete reporting activities:

  • Meet the requirements for turnover, or is a subsidiary of a business which meets those requirements
  • Uses the specified forest risk commodities within its UK commercial activity

If your business uses levels of relevant commodities that are below the specified exemption threshold, you may be eligible to notify/file for an exemption.

Find out who needs to report their forest risk commodities due diligence.

Businesses within the scope of this legislation can use this service to:

  • File and amend exemptions
  • Submit annual reports detailing evidence of due diligence activity

Before you start

Make sure that you have:

  • Reviewed the guidance about when to report and what information you will need to provide
  • Collected the evidence required to submit your exemption or annual report
